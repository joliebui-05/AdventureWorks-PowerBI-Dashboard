# AdventureWorks-PowerBI-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing AdventureWorks sales, products, and customer performance.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>1. Business Understanding</strong></summary>

<br>

Before developing the dashboard, I defined the reporting requirements by identifying the target users, understanding their business objectives, and determining the key questions the dashboard needed to answer.

### Target Users

- Executive Leadership
- Sales Managers
- Product Managers
- Regional Managers
- Marketing Managers

**Primary Users**

Executive Leadership and Sales Management.

### Business Objectives

The dashboard was designed to support the following business goals:

- Increase revenue
- Increase profitability
- Improve product performance
- Grow customer value
- Identify regional opportunities
- Monitor product returns
- Support data-driven decision-making

### Key Business Questions

The dashboard was built to answer questions such as:

- How is the business performing over time?
- Which products and categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which regions perform best and require further attention?
- Where are the strongest growth opportunities?
- Which products have the highest return rates?

### Reporting Frequency

The dashboard supports different reporting needs across the organization:

| Stakeholder | Reporting Frequency |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Executive Leadership | Monthly |
| Sales Managers | Weekly |
| Product & Regional Managers | Weekly |
| Marketing Managers | Monthly |

The reporting structure provides executives with strategic performance monitoring while enabling operational teams to track business performance on a weekly basis.
